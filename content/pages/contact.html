We’d love to hear from you — your thoughts, suggestions, or experiences with diabetes and the BC health care system. There are three ways to do this:

  • Comment on a post or page via Disqus. (You will have to create an account if you don’t have one already).

  • Email us at info@diabeticsbc.ca.

  • Join our Discussion List and dialogue with others who have or are involved with diabetes. We will only use your email address to reply to you.
